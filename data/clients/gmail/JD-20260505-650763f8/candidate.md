# Salesforce Platform Manager

## About This Role
We're seeking a Senior Salesforce Platform Manager to lead the oversight and management of our Salesforce ecosystem. You'll play a critical role in ensuring the health, scalability, and integrity of our platform while architecting a modern development strategy that empowers our teams.

## What You'll Do
- Serve as the primary custodian of Salesforce environments, ensuring production stability and business continuity
- Architect and implement a modern, federated development strategy across the organization
- Establish and lead a Center of Excellence to enable product teams to deploy code safely and efficiently
- Manage the full lifecycle of Salesforce releases, including major seasonal updates
- Oversee continuous CI/CD pipelines using industry-leading tools
- Streamline release management and testing environments across Salesforce, Agentforce, and Data Cloud
- Translate complex technical releases into clear, business-friendly roadmaps
- Apply best practices and design patterns to platform applications
- Continuously grow your technical skills and Salesforce platform expertise

## Required Qualifications
- 5+ years of experience with Salesforce platform management and development
- Strong expertise in Salesforce architecture and multi-tenant architecture principles
- Hands-on experience with CI/CD tools and release orchestration (Copado experience preferred)
- Proficiency in Salesforce development: custom objects, workflows, triggers, SOQL, SOSL
- Experience with Apex, Visualforce, and Lightning (Aura and LWC)
- Deep understanding of Salesforce governor limits and performance optimization
- Strong technical communication and problem-solving abilities
- Experience managing Business Critical production environments

## Preferred Qualifications
- Salesforce Data Cloud experience
- Salesforce Premier Support tier experience
- Experience establishing governance frameworks and Centers of Excellence
