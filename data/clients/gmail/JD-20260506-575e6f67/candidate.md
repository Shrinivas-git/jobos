# Salesforce Platform Manager

## About the Role
We're seeking a Senior Salesforce Platform Manager to lead the oversight and strategic management of our Salesforce ecosystem. In this role, you'll serve as the primary custodian of our environments, ensuring production stability while architecting modern, scalable solutions.

## Key Responsibilities
- Oversee the health, scalability, and release integrity of our Salesforce platform
- Architect a modern, federated development strategy
- Establish and lead a Center of Excellence to empower product teams to deploy code safely and efficiently
- Manage the complete lifecycle of Salesforce releases, including major seasonal updates
- Manage continuous CI/CD pipelines using industry-leading tools
- Streamline release management and testing environments across Salesforce, Agentforce, and Data Cloud
- Translate complex technical releases into clear, business-friendly roadmaps
- Apply best practices and design patterns to platform applications
- Manage Business Critical production environments with confidence

## Required Skills & Experience
- 5+ years of relevant experience in Salesforce platform management
- Expertise in Salesforce Architecture and Platform Management
- Advanced knowledge of CI/CD orchestration and release management
- Strong understanding of Salesforce multi-tenant architecture and Governor limits
- Proven experience with environment management and production support
- Hands-on experience with custom objects, workflows, triggers, SOQL, and SOSL
- Excellent technical communication and problem-solving skills
- Ability to work independently with minimal supervision

## Desired Skills
- Experience with Data Cloud ingestion, modeling, and activation
- Salesforce certifications (Development Lifecycle & Deployment Designer, Platform App Builder, or Admin)
- Knowledge of Apex, Visualforce, and Lightning frameworks (LWC, Aura)
- Background in declarative and configuration-driven development

## Compensation & Benefits
Competitive salary commensurate with experience.

## What We're Looking For
A senior-level professional who combines deep Salesforce technical expertise with strong architectural thinking and business acumen. You should be comfortable taking ownership of critical systems and inspiring teams to achieve operational excellence.
