# Full Stack Developer

Are you a passionate and skilled Full Stack Developer looking to make a significant impact? We are seeking an experienced developer (L2) to join our dynamic team and contribute to building robust and scalable applications. You will be instrumental in developing both frontend and backend solutions, working with modern technologies, and collaborating within an Agile environment.

**Key Responsibilities:**
*   Design, develop, and maintain high-quality frontend and backend applications.
*   Build responsive and intuitive user interfaces using leading JavaScript frameworks (React, Angular, Vue).
*   Develop, implement, and manage secure and scalable APIs and backend services.
*   Work with various database systems (e.g., MySQL, MongoDB, PostgreSQL) to ensure efficient data management.
*   Perform debugging, testing, and optimization to ensure application performance and reliability.
*   Actively participate in an Agile development process, collaborating with cross-functional teams.
*   Utilize modern development tools and practices including Git, CI/CD pipelines, Docker, and cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP).
*   Implement best practices for security, scalability, and performance in all development efforts.
*   Conduct code reviews and provide mentorship and support to junior developers.

**Required Skills & Experience:**
*   **Experience:** 3-7 years of professional experience in full-stack development.
*   **Frontend Expertise:** Strong proficiency in React.js, Angular, or Vue.js, along with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and TypeScript.
*   **Backend Expertise:** Solid experience with backend technologies such as Node.js, Python, Java, or .NET, and developing REST APIs.
*   **Database Management:** Hands-on experience with relational (e.g., MySQL, PostgreSQL) and NoSQL (e.g., MongoDB) databases.
*   **DevOps & Cloud:** Familiarity with Git, CI/CD practices, Docker, and cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or GCP.
*   **Computer Science Fundamentals:** Strong understanding of Object-Oriented Programming (OOP), Data Structures, and System Design.
*   **Methodologies:** Experience working in an Agile development environment.
*   **Security:** Awareness and practical application of Authentication, Authorization, and API Security principles.
