# Salesforce Platform Manager

## About This Role
We're seeking an experienced Salesforce Platform Manager to lead and oversee the health, scalability, and integrity of our Salesforce ecosystem. This is a senior-level position offering significant technical leadership and strategic impact.

## What You'll Do
- Oversee the overall health and scalability of our Salesforce platform
- Serve as the primary custodian of our Salesforce environments, ensuring production stability
- Architect a modern, federated development strategy aligned with business needs
- Establish a Center of Excellence to enable product teams to deploy code safely and efficiently
- Manage the complete lifecycle of Salesforce releases, including major seasonal updates
- Manage continuous integration and continuous deployment (CI/CD) pipelines
- Support streamlining of release management and testing environments across Salesforce, Agentforce, and Data Cloud
- Build and optimize custom Salesforce solutions using best practices and design patterns
- Work independently with minimal supervision
- Translate complex technical releases into clear, business-friendly roadmaps for stakeholders

## What We're Looking For
- **5+ years of experience** in Salesforce Platform Management or Salesforce Architecture
- Expert-level proficiency with Copado for CI/CD and release orchestration
- Deep understanding of Salesforce multi-tenant architecture and governor limits
- Proven experience managing Business Critical production environments
- Strong technical foundation with Salesforce development:
  - Custom objects, Workflows, and Triggers
  - SOQL and SOSL expertise
  - Force.com platform development
- Excellent technical communication skills
- Ability to work independently and manage complex technical initiatives

## Nice-to-Have Qualifications
- Salesforce Certified Development Lifecycle and Deployment Designer
- Salesforce Certified Platform App Builder or Admin certification
- Hands-on experience with Data Cloud
- Apex, Visualforce, or Lightning framework development
- Experience with declarative and configuration-driven development approaches

## Compensation & Benefits
- **Salary:** Competitive

## Work Environment
This role offers the opportunity to work at a senior technical level, directly influencing platform strategy and team enablement in a dynamic, fast-paced environment.
